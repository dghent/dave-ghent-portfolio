Kohort

I joined a product incubator to explore potential market opportunities in several industries. Kohort was spun out during the remote work peak of the pandemic. We solved for remote work collaboration and meeting effectiveness.

Shipped designs

We launched a recording-first experience for remote teams, enabling the creation of team meeting rooms, the addition of topics before and during meetings, and a timeline visualizer to track participant engagement. A shareable recording viewer ensures easy access to all rich recorded content.

Process

I set up a lean framework to ideate based on how might we statements and the lean product canvas. We ran several rounds of this before we narrowed on the final product.

Impact

I was the sole designer on a 6 person team running product design, research, and branding. We launched an experimental product in private beta but didn't secure funding beyond our seed round.

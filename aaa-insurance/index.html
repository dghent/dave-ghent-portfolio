AAA Auto Insurance Quoter

Context

AAA needed to redesign and rebuild their legacy auto insurance quoter, allowing users to adjust coverage options for a price quote. Pivotal was hired to develop the product while also introducing lean design and development practices.

Outcome

As the product design lead on a team of 4 designers and 12 engineers, I helped ship the product before AAA took it in-house for maintenance. I ran a large qualitative study and led the visual direction, including an updated responsive component system.

Research and feedback

I conducted a study to identify pain points in the existing product. With a large stakeholder group, I frequently invited them to our office for design reviews to keep them engaged and aligned.

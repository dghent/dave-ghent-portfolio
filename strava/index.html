Strava

Context

Strava is a social platform for fitness and activity tracking, offering a subscription with premium tools to help cyclists, runners, and swimmers train for races and enhance their performance.

Outcome

I joined as a design lead to work on premium features for performance tracking. I redesigned the paid experience and launched several mobile and web-based performance training tools within the subscription bundle.

Existing feature research

I conducted several studies to define a new persona: the high-performing athlete. I regularly explored how athletes used Strava to meet their unique training needs.

Tracking effort over time

I created a dedicated Summit tab as the new home for paid training features, where Strava users can easily view their activity summaries and track performance improvements over time.

Color system

I designed a new color system, categorized by sport theme, to enhance the visualization of tracked activities over weeks and months.

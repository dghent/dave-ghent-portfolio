Harley-Davidson Ride Planner

Context

At Pivotal, we worked with Harley-Davidson to develop a product that helps riders connect with their community. I was the product design lead on a team of 3 designers and 8 engineers.

Outcome

We built an MVP mobile app for riders to share routes. Harley-Davidson took it in-house, added turn-by-turn navigation, and later submitted it to the app store.

Product validation

I developed a system to track qualitative insights while exploring potential product areas. Prioritizing speed, we gradually increased fidelity as we tested new concepts with motorcycle riders.
